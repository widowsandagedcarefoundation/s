  • Vision
To create a society where widows, orphans, and the elderly are empowered, cared for, and given opportunities to lead fulfilling lives.
The "Let the Widow and Aged Smile Foundation" is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting widows, orphans, and the elderly by providing them with the care, empowerment, and resources needed for a quality life. This foundation aims to fill the gaps in social welfare systems, particularly in marginalized communities where these vulnerable groups face neglect and challenges. By creating programs for care, empowerment, and sustainable support, the foundation seeks to restore dignity and joy to their lives. The slogan "Let the Widow and Aged Smile" embodies the essence of our mission, to bring hope and happiness to those in need.
To support widows, orphans, and the elderly through quality care, empowerment programs, and sustainable development initiatives that foster dignity and happiness.
